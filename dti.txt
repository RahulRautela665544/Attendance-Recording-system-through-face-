8.Describe Your Product.

This is like answering someone's question. "What are you doing as a Design thinking and innovation Project" or "What is your Project" or "Can you tell something about your Project". Please write a Minimum 500 and Maximum 1000 Characters. 
This Question Carries One Mark.

Our Design Thinking and Innovation project is about building a modern attendance system using face recognition. In many colleges, attendance is still taken through QR codes. Because of this, some students share the code with their friends, and attendance gets marked even if they are not present in class. Since attendance is important for internal marks, this creates problems and makes the system unfair.

To fix this issue, we are creating a system that checks students by recognizing their faces and confirming their presence through simple head movements. This helps ensure that only students who are actually sitting in the classroom can mark their attendance. The system also saves the time of entry, which makes the record more clear and trustworthy. It works properly even if students are wearing masks or glasses.

The main purpose of our project is to make attendance more honest, accurate, and secure. Through this project, we want to reduce false attendance, improve discipline, and support teachers and institutions in managing attendance in a better way.

By combining affordability, live verification, adaptability, and strong privacy measures, our project offers a more practical and reliable attendance solution compared to existing systems.
